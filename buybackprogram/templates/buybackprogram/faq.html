Frequently Asked Questions
Where can I sell my items?

Each program has a system and a structure mentioned on the program page. The manager for this program will most likely only accept items if the contract has been created at this exact location.

How can I sell my items?

  • Have your items at the program location
  • Click Use on the program you wish to use
  • Copy paste your items from your inventory into the calculator
  • Follow the settings given by the calculator when creating the contract

How can I disable discord notifications for my contracts?

You can disable notifications about your contracts via the My Settings page
